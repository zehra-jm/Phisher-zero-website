// ABOUT

The Threat Is Real.
So Is The Defense.

Phishing attacks exploit human psychology — not software bugs. Millions of fake sites go live every year. Traditional blacklists miss zero-day domains for hours after launch. Phisher Zero changes the game with a multi-agent AI pipeline that reasons about deceptive intent, not just known patterns.

🎭

Social Engineering

Attackers impersonate banks, universities, and services. Our NLP Agent understands tone, urgency cues, and manipulation tactics — not just keywords.

🔗

Malicious URLs

Zero-day phishing domains bypass blacklists for hours. Our URL Agent applies 8 structural heuristics and cross-references 70+ global threat vendors.

🧠

AI Ensemble

No single model is enough. Four specialized agents pool their signals with a weighted ensemble and a red-flag override for confirmed threats.

💬

Plain-English Verdicts

Most tools give you a red icon and leave you guessing. Phisher Zero explains exactly why something is dangerous — in language anyone can understand.
